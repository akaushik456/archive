Implementation Considerations

This bot combines the intrigue of time capsules with the dynamic world of Web3, offering both utility and an entertaining twist on how we think about time and value in the digital age.

  • Security

    The core of this bot's functionality is security and trust. Ensure robust encryption and never hold private keys. The bot should only manage the unlocking mechanism.

  • Legal and Compliance

    Understand and comply with regulations regarding digital assets, especially in terms of holding funds in escrow or gifting crypto.

  • User Interface

    Make the process of setting up capsules intuitive, with clear instructions on how to claim or view content after the time lock.
